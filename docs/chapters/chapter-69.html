1.1 There is an old saying:

1.2 "It is better to become the passive

1.3 in order to see what will happen.

1.4 It is better to retreat a foot

1.5 than to advance only an inch."

2.1 This is called

2.2 being flexible while advancing,

2.3 pushing back without using force,

2.4 and destroying the enemy without engaging him.

3.1 There is no greater disaster

3.2 than underestimating your enemy.

3.3 Underestimating your enemy

3.4 means losing your greatest assets.

3.5 When equal forces meet in battle,

3.6 victory will go to the one

3.7 that enters with the greatest sorrow.
