1.1 A good traveler leaves no tracks,

1.2 and a skillful speaker is well rehearsed.

1.3 A good bookkeeper has an excellent memory,

1.4 and a well made door is easy to open and needs no locks.

1.5 A good knot needs no rope and it can not come undone.

2.1 Thus the Master is willing to help everyone,

2.2 and doesn't know the meaning of rejection.

2.3 She is there to help all of creation,

2.4 and doesn't abandon even the smallest creature.

2.5 This is called embracing the light.

3.1 What is a good person but a bad person's teacher?

3.2 What is a bad person but raw material for his teacher?

3.3 If you fail to honor your teacher or fail to enjoy your student,

3.4 you will become deluded no matter how smart you are.

3.5 It is the secret of prime importance.

Tao Te Ching, A Modern Paraphrase (1996).
