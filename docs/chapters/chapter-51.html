1.1 The Tao gives birth to all of creation.

1.2 The virtue of Tao in nature nurtures them,

1.3 and their family gives them their form.

1.4 Their environment then shapes them into completion.

1.5 That is why every creature honors the Tao and its virtue.

2.1 No one tells them to honor the Tao and its virtue,

2.2 it happens all by itself.

2.3 So the Tao gives them birth,

2.4 and its virtue cultivates them,

2.5 cares for them,

2.6 nurtures them,

2.7 gives them a place of refuge and peace,

2.8 helps them to grow and shelters them.

3.1 It gives them life without wanting to posses them,

3.2 and cares for them expecting nothing in return.

3.3 It is their master, but it does not seek to dominate them.

3.4 This is called the dark and mysterious virtue.
