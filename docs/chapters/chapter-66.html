1.1 Rivers and seas are rulers

1.2 of the streams of hundreds of valleys

1.3 because of the power of their low position.

2.1 If you want to be the ruler of people,

2.2 you must speak to them like you are their servant.

2.3 If you want to lead other people,

2.4 you must put their interest ahead of your own.

3.1 The people will not feel burdened,

3.2 if a wise person is in a position of power.

3.3 The people will not feel like they are being manipulated,

3.4 if a wise person is in front as their leader.

3.5 The whole world will ask for her guidance,

3.6 and will never get tired of her.

3.7 Because she does not like to compete,

3.8 no one can compete with the things she accomplishes.
