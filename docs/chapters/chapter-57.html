1.1 Govern your country with integrity,

1.2 Weapons of war can be used with great cunning,

1.3 but loyalty is only won by not-doing.

1.4 How do I know the way things are?

1.5 By these:

2.1 The more prohibitions you make,

2.2 the poorer people will be.

2.3 The more weapons you posses,

2.4 the greater the chaos in your country.

2.5 The more knowledge that is acquired,

2.6 the stranger the world will become.

2.7 The more laws that you make,

2.8 the greater the number of criminals.

3.1 Therefore the Master says:

3.2 I do nothing,

3.3 and people become good by themselves.

3.4 I seek peace,

3.5 and people take care of their own problems.

3.6 I do not meddle in their personal lives,

3.7 and the people become prosperous.

3.8 I let go of all my desires,

3.9 and the people return to the Uncarved Block.
