1.1 There is nothing better than moderation

2.1 Those who follow the Tao early

2.2 will have an abundance of virtue.

2.7 When you know the Mother of the kingdom,

2.8 then you will be long enduring.

3.2 the Way to a long life and great spiritual vision.
