1.1 Small countries with few people are best.

1.2 Give them all of the things they want,

1.3 and they will see that they do not need them.

1.4 Teach them that death is a serious thing,

1.5 and to be content to never leave their homes.

1.6 Even though they have plenty

1.7 of horses, wagons and boats,

1.8 they won't feel that they need to use them.

1.9 Even if they have weapons and shields,

1.10 they will keep them out of sight.

1.11 Let people enjoy the simple technologies,

1.12 let them enjoy their food,

1.13 let them make their own clothes,

1.14 let them be content with their own homes,

1.15 and delight in the customs that they cherish.

1.16 Although the next country is close enough

1.17 that they can hear their roosters crowing and dogs barking,

1.18 they are content never to visit each other

1.19 all of the days of their life.
