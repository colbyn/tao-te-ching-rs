1.1 She who follows the way of the Tao

1.2 will draw the world to her steps.

1.3 She can go without fear of being injured,

1.4 because she has found peace and tranquility in her heart.

2.1 Where there is music and good food,

2.2 people will stop to enjoy it.

2.3 But words spoken of the Tao

2.4 seem to them boring and stale.

2.5 When looked at, there is nothing for them to see.

2.6 When listen for, there is nothing for them to hear.

2.7 Yet if they put it to use, it would never be exhausted.
