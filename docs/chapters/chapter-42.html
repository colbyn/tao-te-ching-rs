1.1 The Tao gave birth to One.

1.2 The One gave birth to Two.

1.3 The Two gave birth to Three.

1.4 The Three gave birth to all of creation.

2.1 All things carry Yin

2.2 yet embrace Yang.

2.3 They blend their life breaths

2.4 in order to produce harmony.

3.2 But the noble ones take these as their titles.

3.3 In losing, much is gained,

3.4 and in gaining, much is lost.

4.2 "The strong and violent will not die a natural death."
