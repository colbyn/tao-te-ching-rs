1.1 A large country should take the low place like a great watershed,

1.2 which from its low position assumes the female role.

1.3 The female overcomes the male by the power of her position.

1.4 Her tranquility gives rise to her humility.

2.1 If a large country takes the low position,

2.2 it will be able to influence smaller countries.

2.3 If smaller countries take the lower position,

2.4 then they can allow themselves to be influenced.

2.5 So both seek to take the lower position

2.6 in order to influence the other, or be influenced.

3.1 Large countries should desire to protect and help the people,

3.2 and small countries should desire to serve others.

3.3 Both large and small countries benefit greatly from humility.

Tao Te Ching, A Modern Paraphrase (1996).
