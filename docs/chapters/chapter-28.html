1.1 Know the masculine,

1.2 but keep to the feminine:

1.3 and become a watershed to the world.

1.4 If you embrace the world,

1.5 the Tao will never leave you

1.6 and you become as a little child.

2.1 Know the white,

2.2 yet keep to the black:

2.3 be a model for the world.

2.4 If you are a model for the world,

2.5 the Tao inside you will strengthen

2.6 and you will return whole to your eternal beginning.

3.1 Know the honorable,

3.2 but do not shun the disgraced:

3.3 embracing the world as it is.

3.4 If you embrace the world with compassion,

3.5 then your virtue will return you to the Uncarved Block.

4.1 The block of wood is carved into utensils

4.2 by carving void into the wood.

4.3 The Master uses the utensils, yet prefers to keep to the block

4.4 because of its limitless possibilities.

4.5 Great works do not involve discarding substance.
